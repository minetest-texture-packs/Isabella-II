--ABOUT--

Isabella II is a reworked version of [i]Isabella[/i] (now called [i]Isabella Classic[/i].) It offers the same melancholic feel but with brighter visuals, improving the experience on a wider range of monitors.

Minecraft Forum Thread: http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/242175-Isabella/"
